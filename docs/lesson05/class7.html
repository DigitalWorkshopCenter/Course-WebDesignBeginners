Hands-on Exercise
SEO

Overview

In this activity, you will incorporate SEO principles into a webpage.

Instructions

  1. Find a webpage in your lab project that you are working on. This webpage should be further along and close to completion if not completed already. Pick one of your pages that has 5 images and several other HTML elements.
  2. Open this webpage with live server.
  3. Navigate to this webpage. Ignore step 1 :) and focus on steps 2-5 while you seek to optimize this webpage.

    • Create titles and descriptions for each page

    • Utilize anchor text

    • Add alt text to all your images

    • Give your site structure with the right headers

Conclusion

Congratulations! You now know several techniques for optimizing web pages for search engines. Know that when you have a website in production, you can check your efficacy in these techniques with tools similar to this one.
